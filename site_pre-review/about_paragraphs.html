About Us

Making UX Research Accessible

We’re a UX research and playtesting platform built to support both game studios and tech teams with affordable, high-quality research solutions. Whether you're fine-tuning a blockbuster title, launching a new app, or validating a product concept, we believe every team deserves real user insights.

Built for Builders. Powered by Passionate People.

We take care of the research logistics—recruitment, setup, testing, analysis—so creators can focus on what they do best. From early-stage discovery to post-launch refinement, we make user feedback fast, actionable, and stress-free.

Empowering the Next Generation of Researchers

We’re more than a platform. We’re a launchpad for the next wave of UX researchers. Our model gives early-career professionals hands-on experience with real products, real users, and real impact. Every project supports both product development and researcher growth.


Our Story

Founded in 2025 in Orlando, Florida

After seeing products released time and time again without adequate user experience research and testing, we came together and decided to take it upon ourselves to help those struggling with understanding their users. No matter how great your product is, if your potential users can't figure out how to use it, then they will take their precious time elsewhere.
