Frequently Asked Questions

What is UFI Research?
We are a user experience (UX) research group that helps connect clients with testers to gather feedback on products that are launched or in development. For more information, please go to our About Page
How do I sign up?
You can participate in tests by filling out our Participant Form
Participation surveys are sent out when new tests become available. All you have to do is fill out the surveys to the best of your ability and a researcher will reach out to you if you are selected.
Will I get compensated?
Yes! All of our testers get compensated for the tests they participate in, some tests may pay more than others depending on the type and the length of the study.
Do I need to go somewhere to test?
The majority of our tests will be done remotely from your home. All you need is your computer or mobile device to participate. If a test requires you to be in-person, you will know before signing up.
Can I talk about the test I participated in?
Sharing anything about the test you participated in, including video, audio, images, or text, is strictly prohibited. All participants must sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) prior to testing. Violations may lead to a platform ban and legal action.

Please reach out to our research team with any additional questions!
