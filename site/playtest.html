Frequently Asked Questions

What is UFI Research?
We are a user experience research group that helps connect studios with testers to gather feedback on products that are launched or in development.
What do I need to test?
You can participate in tests by joining our tester community here. We will send out recruitment surveys when new tests are available and if your experience matches what the sudio is looking for then we will reach out to you!
Will I get compensated?
Yes! All of our testers get compensated for the tests they participate in, some tests may pay more than others depending on the type and the length of the study.
Do I need to go somewhere to test?
Most of our tests happen remote, all you need is your home computer, console, or mobile device (depending on the test requirements) to participate.
I was signed up to test but my availability changed, what do I do?
No worries! Reach out to one of our team members via Discord or email right away to get rescheduled or to cancel your session.
Can I talk about the test I participated in?
Sharing anything about the test you participated in is strictly forbidden. No video, image, voice recording, or text recording can be shared with anyone before, during, or after the study. Any violation of our terms could result in receiving a ban from the platform as well as legal action.
How can I sign up?
You can participate in tests by joining our tester community here [LINK TO DISCORD]. Recruitment surveys are sent when new tests are available. If your experience matches what the studio is looking for, we will reach out to you!
What is playtesting?
Games, apps, and tech products are always looking for user feedback! Playtesting is a method where a user interacts with a product and a researcher records certain aspects of the experience. Playtesting products may involve answering questions, playing games, and/or interacting with apps.
What are the requirements to participate?
Most tests will be completed in the comfort of your home! The requirements vary from study to study, but most require either a mobile phone, home computer, or console. Additionally, some tests may require a controller based on the test requirements.
What happens during a test?
Once you show interest in a study, one of our researchers will reach out to you and ask a few questions regarding the test (experience, availability, ect.). If you fit the requirements for a study, we will schedule a time to conduct the test. All participants are required to sign an NDA prior to the test and after the test, we may send you a survey on the study you participated in. Once everything has been completed, we will send you compensation within two weeks following the study.
Can I discuss the test I participated in?
No. Discussing the contents of the test you participated in is strictly forbidden. Prior to each test, testers are required to sign and NDA. No video, image, voice recording, or text recording can be shared with anyone before, during, or after the study. Violating these terms could result in a ban from the platform as well as legal action.
