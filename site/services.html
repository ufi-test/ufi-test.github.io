Services Offered

Usability

How it Works: If you’re unsure where your usability issues lie, we conduct a deep dive into how users interact with your product. We observe their behavior to uncover friction points that could affect their ability to learn or use your product.
Why it Benefits You: You’ll get specific insights into areas where users struggle, helping you improve learnability and ensure smoother interactions. This ultimately enhances your product’s overall appeal and accessibility.

Playtest

How it Works: Our team guides users through a selected portion of your game or product, gathering detailed feedback on their thoughts, emotions, and behavior as they interact with it.
Why it Benefits You: You’ll gain rich, user-centered feedback that highlights what players love, what frustrates them, and where the experience could be enhanced. It’s a great way to fine-tune the product before launch or make improvements after release.

Expert Analysis

How it Works: Our team of expert researchers conduct an in-depth evaluation of your product’s user experience. We look for pain points and issues that might not be immediately obvious, drawing on years of industry knowledge and past experience.
Why it Benefits You: You’ll get expert-level insights into subtle usability issues that could be holding back your product’s potential. Our analysis often uncovers issues that teams miss, helping you make informed decisions.

Diary Study

How it Works: We follow users over an extended period as they engage with your product in their natural environment. By documenting their experience over time, we uncover long-term usage patterns, challenges, and emotional responses.
Why it Benefits You: You’ll gain a comprehensive understanding of your product’s impact on users in the real world, revealing both immediate pain points and ongoing challenges. This deep insight helps you optimize your product for sustained success.

Concept Testing

How it Works: Before committing to full development, we gather feedback from users on your product concept, prototype, or idea. Through structured testing and analysis, we gauge their initial reactions and identify areas for improvement.
Why it Benefits You: By testing your ideas early, you save time and resources by validating the core concept with real users. This ensures you’re on the right track before investing heavily in production.

Focus Groups

How it Works: We bring together a diverse group of users to discuss and interact with your product, guided by researchers who ask insightful questions and observe key behaviors. This collaborative environment provides an abundance of qualitative data.
Why it Benefits You: Focus groups allow you to understand how users think and feel about your product in real-time. The discussions provide actionable insights into user motivations, preferences, and pain points, helping you refine the product for better engagement.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical study take?
Timelines vary by method - some studies may be done in as little as 1-2 weeks, while more in-depth studies may require a longer horizon, possibly spanning weeks to months. Please let us know what your goals are and we will be happy to provide an approximate turn-around time!
What kind of deliverables can I expect?
All studies will include a report, in a format that best presents the acquired data, typically a Word Document, Adobe PDF, or a PowerPoint Presentation. Also, depending on the study, deliverables may include videos, transcripts, user quotes, design recommendations, and competitive comparisons.
How do you recruit participants?
We recruit participants using customized screeners and surveys that are developed with the client and their project in mind.
