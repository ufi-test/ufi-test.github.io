What is User Experience Research?

User Experience Research is the process of understanding how people use and experience products and services.
We focus on gathering feedback directly from people while they interact with a product - these are the “users.” By talking to and observing users, we gain insight into their behaviors and the challenges they may face.
This research process often includes methods like interviews, surveys, usability tests, and direct observation.

What's the point?

The purpose of User Research is to understand whether a product is easy to use, to see if it solves the right problems, and to highlight areas for improvement.
By identifying pain points and gathering feedback early, we help refine products and create a smoother user experience.
