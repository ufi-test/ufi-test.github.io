About Us

Who We Are

We’re a team of UX researchers with a shared mission: make research more accessible and help new talent thrive. Our journey started in university lecture halls, and now we’re more than just peers. We are collaborators, friends, and professionals.

What We Do

We offer a broad selection of methodologies that are customized to your goals. Whether you’re refining a UI or building from scratch, we deliver tailored research that uncovers how people think, feel, and interact with your product—so you have clarity and confidence.

Why It Works

Our approach combines real-world experience with fresh ideas. We’ve worked with indie teams, big studios, and startups. We also provide hands-on opportunities for new UX researchers, bringing fresh perspectives to every study while maintaining high-quality outcomes.

The Launchpad Program

Launchpad is our mentorship-driven research model. Junior UX researchers gain real, hands-on experience by being paired with senior mentors who guide their work. Clients get cost-effective, professional research, while helping support the next wave of UX talent.

A Smarter Investment

We are a smart partner whether you’re launching a new feature or testing an early idea. Choose from our selection of methodologies, or support future UX professionals through our cost-effective mentorship program. We’ll help you move forward with confidence.

We care about your success, let's create an experience that your users won't forget!
